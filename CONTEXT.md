# Personal Academic Website

This context defines the language for Wiwit Suryanto's personal academic website. The site presents personal branding, research portfolio material, and teaching work connected to Geophysics UGM.

## Language

**Personal Academic Website**:
The website for Wiwit Suryanto's professional identity, combining personal branding, research portfolio content, and lecture-related material.
_Avoid_: Generic portfolio, generic personal site

**Personal Branding**:
The coherent public presentation of Wiwit Suryanto's academic identity, credibility, values, and professional focus.
_Avoid_: Self-promotion, marketing page

**Research Portfolio**:
A curated presentation of research interests, projects, publications, outputs, collaborations, and evidence of scholarly contribution.
_Avoid_: Project gallery, work samples

**Publication**:
A scholarly output listed as evidence of research contribution, such as an article, paper, proceedings item, or comparable academic work.
_Avoid_: Post, article card

**Academic Peer**:
A researcher, lecturer, or scholar who evaluates Wiwit Suryanto's expertise, research credibility, and academic fit.
_Avoid_: Visitor, reader

**Research Collaborator**:
A potential or current partner who may connect with Wiwit Suryanto through shared research interests, projects, datasets, or institutional work.
_Avoid_: Client, lead

**Geophysics UGM Student**:
A student who needs lecture-related material, academic guidance, or context for geophysics coursework at Universitas Gadjah Mada.
_Avoid_: Customer, user

**Lecture Work**:
Teaching-related content for Wiwit Suryanto's role in geophysics, including course material, lecture references, and academic guidance.
_Avoid_: Blog posts, tutorials

**Geophysics UGM**:
The academic setting for the site's teaching context, referring to geophysics at Universitas Gadjah Mada.
_Avoid_: Generic university context

## Claim

The LEAF 2026 deck claims that FMIPA UGM ran a 2022-2026 research-management experiment in which the Vice Dean's primary KPI shifted toward industry funding, product adoption, and community impact; the deck reports 82 industrial collaboration projects, Rp 184 billion total contract value, several adopted products, and no decline in publication output.

## Evidence

- The deck names [[wiki/entities/wiwit-suryanto]] as the presenter and Vice Dean for Research and Community Services at [[wiki/entities/fmipa-ugm]].
- The deck defines the unit, period, intervention, measured outcomes, and control variable.
- The deck lists material outputs: SpectraGAMA v4.0, PertaGAMANT v3.0, Tarsius, Geophysical UAV, Ocean Bottom Seismometer, and MinerbaOne.
- The workbook independently supports an 82-row FMIPA UGM industrial/applied project portfolio, although its funding total and year distribution differ from the deck.

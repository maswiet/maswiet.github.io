## Summary

This 9-page PDF slide deck titled "Life Balancing as Faculty staff trough Tridharma Perguruan Tinggi" presents Wiwit Suryanto's view of lecturer life balance as a balance across teaching, impactful research, community service, global professional participation, and industry-facing applied work. It is dated 2023-10-17 in the title slide.

The deck is an earlier, more personal Tridharma framing that overlaps with later sources about research management and FMIPA industrial collaboration. It lists 2022 and 2023 industrial contract portfolios, global/professional service roles, JICA partnership activity, and BMKG earthquake-processing/early-warning system work.

## Key Points

- The presentation frames lecturer balance through `Tridharma Perguruan Tinggi`: teaching, research, and community service.
- Teaching is framed as inspiration for students to develop innovative thinking through multiple teaching media.
- Impactful research examples include a technology mission to Algeria in 2012 and visual references to geophysical tools such as SpectraGAMA and PertaGAMANT.
- Community service is framed as international or global-facing, including a JICA partnership since 2017.
- Professional/global roles listed include Expert Board of the Indonesia Association of Geophysicists for 2022-2024, Regional Advisory Committee Asia-Oceania Geoscience Society for 2023-2025, Coordinator for IRIS-USA, Vice Dean for Research and Community Services FMIPA UGM for 2021-2026, Section Editor of Journal Earthquake, and PIC UGM for the BMKG earthquake processing and early warning system project funded by the World Bank for 2023-2025.
- The 2022 industrial contract slide reports total industrial contracts of `US$633,265.00` and lists nine projects, including underwater seismometer prototype development, SP-ERT injection monitoring, cloud-based passive seismic computation, EAOR, sonochemistry for heavy oil molecules, PertaGAMANT/noise tomography, passive seismic surveys, passive seismic processing, and earthquake monitoring system development.
- The 2023 industrial contract slide reports total industrial contracts of `US$1,097,966.67` and lists projects including SP-ERT, cloud-based passive seismic tools, E-EOR, controlled-source electromagnetic reservoir detection, PertaGAMANT version 2.0, LFPS projects, geotechnical work, machine-learning fault and pore-pressure analysis, and earthquake processing systems.

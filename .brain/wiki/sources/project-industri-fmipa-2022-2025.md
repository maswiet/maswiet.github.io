## Summary

The workbook titled "Industrial Project FMIPA UGM" lists 82 industrial or applied projects associated with FMIPA UGM across rows dated 2022-2026. The portfolio is heavily weighted toward energy-sector collaborations, especially Pertamina/PHE-related entities, and includes passive seismic, seismic processing, reservoir studies, EOR, geophysical software, digital systems, machine learning, UAV/magnetic/LIDAR mapping, MASW/geotechnical work, public-sector digital systems, and a small number of international or non-energy collaborations.

The file name says 2022-2025, but the extracted rows include five 2026 entries. The spreadsheet does not explicitly identify Wiwit Suryanto's role in these projects.

## Key Points

- Extracted workbook structure: one sheet, `Sheet1`, with title row "Industrial Project FMIPA UGM" and columns for number, research topic, industry, funding, and year.
- Extracted records: 82 project rows.
- Year distribution: 9 rows in 2022, 12 in 2023, 18 in 2024, 38 in 2025, and 5 in 2026.
- Funding column total: `180,575,375,943` across 80 rows with numeric funding values; the workbook does not state the currency.
- Largest recorded funding row: "Analisis dan Pengembangan Sistem Digital Terpadu Mineral dan Batubara" for Kementrian ESDM in 2024, with funding value `36,341,988,760`.
- Broad collaborator pattern: 44 rows are in the Pertamina/PHE ecosystem; smaller groups include Indonesian agencies, BMKG, ESDM/Minerba, international or non-Indonesian entities, and other private or industrial partners.
- Dominant technical patterns are passive/seismic acquisition-processing, non-seismic geophysical methods, reservoir/hydrocarbon/EOR work, software and digital systems, machine learning, and broader public-sector or engineering applications.

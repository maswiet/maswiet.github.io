## Summary

Lecturer life balance through Tridharma is the idea that an academic career can be balanced by treating teaching, research, and community service as mutually reinforcing domains rather than competing obligations. In the 2023 PDF, Wiwit Suryanto presents this balance through innovative teaching, impactful research, international community service, professional service roles, and industry-facing applied work.

## Key Points

- Teaching is positioned as a way to inspire student innovation.
- Research impact is shown through international missions, applied geophysical tools, and industry-facing work.
- Community service is not limited to local service; the deck frames it through JICA partnership and global disaster-mitigation engagement.
- Professional service and leadership roles are part of the balance, not separate from it.
- Industrial collaboration appears as an applied-research expression of Tridharma, especially for geophysics, passive seismic work, earthquake monitoring, and reservoir-related projects.

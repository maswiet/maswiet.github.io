Research

Ongoing and recent projects in seismology and volcano geophysics. Each entry summarises the question, my role, and the outputs it has produced.

Active projects

Real-time seismic monitoring of Mount Merapi

Role: Principal investigator Period: 2021 – present With: BPPTKG, GFZ Potsdam
  • Volcano seismology
  • Event classification
  • Hazard

A broadband and short-period network feeding a continuous processing pipeline that detects, classifies, and locates volcano-tectonic and long-period events. The goal is to shorten the time between anomalous seismicity and actionable hazard assessment.

Outputs: 2 journal articles · open event catalog · monitoring dashboard

Ambient noise tomography of central Java

Role: Co-investigator Period: 2020 – present With: ITB, Kyoto University
  • Tomography
  • Crustal structure
  • Basin imaging

Cross-correlating months of continuous noise records to recover surface-wave dispersion and invert for shear-velocity structure, resolving the depth of sedimentary basins that amplify ground shaking near Yogyakarta.

Outputs: 1 journal article · velocity model (open data)

Recent projects

Low-cost seismic sensor network for schools

Role: Lead Period: 2019 – 2022 With: Regional education offices
  • Instrumentation
  • Open hardware
  • Outreach

Designing an affordable geophone-plus-microcontroller station and classroom curriculum, expanding the density of seismic observations while building earthquake awareness among students.

Outputs: hardware guide · 1 proceedings paper · 12 deployed stations
